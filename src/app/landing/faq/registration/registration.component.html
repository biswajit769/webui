General Questions

Q. What is RealTimeMoney?

A. RealTimeMoney is a NBFC lending marketplace where willing Lenders can transact with pre-verified creditworthy Borrowers on short to medium term unsecured personal loans.

Q.Is RealTimeMoney regulated?

A. RealTimeMoney, being a marketplace, does not come under the purview of the RBI.

Q. How can I become a member on RealTimeMoney?

A. You may register on our website as an if you meet the eligibility criteria set by us. Click here to know the eligibility criteria.

Q. Why do I need to register at RealTimeMoney?

A. To become either a Borrower or a Lender, one must register at RealTimeMoney. The registration process involves filling in a simple online application form. Post successful registration, a member can access the RealTimeMoney loan marketplace, and depending on whether the member is a Borrower or a Lender, the member may proceed to create and list a loan or may search for listed loans respectively.

Q. Is there any eligibility criteria to register at RealTimeMoney?

A. A person should be a resident of India and above 21 years of age. The person should also have a valid bank account, a valid PAN number and a valid identity proof and residence proof.

Q.Are there any registration charges to sign up on RealTimeMoney?

A. Registration Fee to be paid during one time Borrower registration is: Rs.1500/- No Registration Fee is required from Lenders.

Q.Why is phone verification necessary?

A.To be sure that a person has entered the correct contact information, RealTimeMoney will verify the phone number provided by the person during registration. This will help us to serve our members in a more timely and efficient manner. Please note that user details will remain confidential as per the RealTimeMoney Terms & Conditions.
