What is peer-to-peer lending?

Peer-to-peer lending is a new method of financing debt that allows people to borrow and lend money without the intervention of traditional financial intermediaries, such as banks or private moneylenders. Peer-to-peer lending helps connect creditworthy borrowers to lenders to enable quick, hassle-free, and cost-effective loans. Peer-to-peer lending has grown rapidly in recent years and is fast emerging as a preferred source of fixed income for investors.

Given the low returns currently offered on historically “safe” fixed deposits, and the relatively high risk exposure investors must bear to generate moderate returns on equity investments, peer-to-peer lending offers an attractive investment opportunity to achieve high returns at moderate risk to investors.
